Our Courses

Playgroup

A gentle introduction to the world of learning. Our Playgroup program focuses on sensory exploration, social interaction, and fun-filled activities that help toddlers feel safe, loved, and curious in a warm environment.

Nursery

Nursery develops foundational skills through engaging activities focused on speech, colors, shapes, numbers, and letters. Music, movement, and hands-on learning build confidence, language, and motor skills in a joyful environment.

Junior KG

Junior Kindergarten focuses on pre-writing, phonics, number skills, and motor development. Children grow in confidence as they learn through structured play, group activities, and independent tasks that prepare them for school life.

Senior KG

Our Senior KG program prepares children for formal school with reading readiness, basic math, language skills, and life values. Focused attention on communication, discipline, and creativity ensures a smooth transition to Grade 1.
