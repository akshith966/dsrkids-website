Courses

Playgroup

A gentle introduction to the world of learning. Our Playgroup program focuses on sensory exploration, social interaction, and fun-filled activities that help toddlers feel safe, loved, and curious in a warm environment.

Nursery

Nursery develops foundational skills through engaging activities focused on speech, colors, shapes, numbers, and letters. Music, movement, and hands-on learning build confidence, language, and motor skills in a joyful environment.

Junior kg

Junior Kindergarten focuses on pre-writing, phonics, number skills, and motor development. Children grow in confidence as they learn through structured play, group activities, and independent tasks that prepare them for school life.

Senior Kg

Our Senior KG program prepares children for formal school with reading readiness, basic math, language skills, and life values. Focused attention on communication, discipline, and creativity ensures a smooth transition to Grade 1.

Facilities

At Sri Dsr Kids Playschool, we understand that safety and emotional security are essential for a child’s growth. Our premises are clean, child-friendly, and fully monitored to ensure complete protection at all times. Trained teachers and support staff supervise every activity with love and care. Entry and exit are strictly managed, and health protocols are always followed. We create a warm, homely atmosphere where every child feels comfortable, confident, and safe — giving parents peace of mind every single day.

At Sri Dsr Kids Playschool, we follow the Montessori method to encourage independence, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning. Children explore at their own pace using specially designed materials that build confidence and curiosity. Our classrooms are calm, child-centered environments where teachers guide rather than instruct, allowing kids to discover through hands-on experience. This method nurtures not only academic growth but also social and emotional development, laying a strong foundation for each child’s future success

At Sri DSR Kids Playschool, festivals are celebrated with great joy, color, and learning. From Diwali to Christmas, Pongal to Eid, each occasion is marked with special decorations, cultural activities, and traditional dress. Children engage in songs, dances, and art projects that help them understand the meaning and values behind every festival. These celebrations promote unity, respect for diversity, and excitement among the little ones. Parents are often invited to join the festivities, making it a truly joyful and inclusive experience for all.

At Sri DSR Kids Playschool, learning goes beyond classrooms with exciting spaces like the splash pool, AV room, and playroom. Our splash pool offers a fun, safe way for children to enjoy water play while building motor skills. The AV room introduces kids to interactive learning through stories, rhymes, and educational videos. In the colorful playroom, children explore toys, puzzles, and pretend play that boost creativity and social interaction. These engaging environments make every day joyful, active, and full of meaningful discoveries for our little stars.
