About Sri DSR Kids Playschool

At Sri DSR Kids Playschool, we believe every child deserves a joyful and meaningful start to their learning journey. Founded by passionate educators and parents, our school was created to be more than just a place for early education — it’s a home away from home.

Our mission is to foster curiosity, independence, and confidence in children through a nurturing environment rooted in Montessori-inspired methods, hands-on activities, and joyful play. Every day, our children explore language, numbers, stories, movement, music, and art — all designed to match their developmental stages.

We value every child’s uniqueness. Our classrooms are warm, interactive, and safe. Our teachers are trained to guide gently and attentively, helping children discover who they are and what they love. With small teacher-student ratios, personal care, and parent engagement, we ensure holistic growth in every little one.

Whether your child joins in Play Group, Nursery, LKG, or UKG, we promise they will grow with confidence, creativity, and happiness.

Join the DSR Family — where tiny hands build bright futures!
